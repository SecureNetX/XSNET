SecureNet Whitepaper

Introduction

SecureNet is an innovative blockchain-based meme network that enables secure and encrypted meme communication. Our goal is to create a digital infrastructure that is completely protected from surveillance and external attacks.

Tokenomics

The total supply of SecureNet tokens (SNET) is 600 billion. A burn fee of 0.3% is applied to every transaction, ensuring a gradual reduction in total supply and increased scarcity over time.
